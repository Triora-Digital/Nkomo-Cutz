The Story

WHERE IT
ALL STARTED.

Nkomo Cutz started with one chair, four walls, and brick on every side. A garage with character. A barber with standards. And a simple promise: every client walks out looking clean — for $20, no exceptions.

There's no complicated pricing structure here. No awkward upsells mid-cut. No tiers based on hair length or style. That's not how it works at Nkomo Cutz. You come in, you describe your cut, and you get the best version of it.

"THE GARAGE IS THE BRAND. RAW WALLS, REAL CRAFT, ZERO PRETENSE."

From the first fade to the five-hundredth, the standard hasn't moved. That's the whole thing.
