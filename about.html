The Story

WHERE IT
ALL STARTED.

Nkomo Cutz started with one chair, four walls, and a garage door left open to the street. The walls are salmon render. The floor is the same red brick paving you see out front. And inside those four walls, something real was built.

No fancy fitout. No inflated prices. No complicated menu. Just a barber who understood that the best cut someone ever got shouldn't cost more than $20.

"THE GARAGE IS THE BRAND. REAL WALLS, REAL CRAFT, ZERO PRETENSE."

From the first fade to the hundredth, the standard has never moved. Every client leaves looking clean. That's the whole deal.
